## Sleep & water framework (locked — applies to every weekly plan)

### Sleep — two tracks aligned with the office schedule:

- **Track A** (office days, 3x/week — Tue/Wed/Thu): in bed by 11:00 PM, up by 5:00 AM (~6 hrs).
- **Track B** (non-office days, 4x/week): in bed by 12:00 AM (midnight), up by 8:00 AM (~8 hrs).
- Weekly average: ~7.1 hrs/night. Floor: weekly average ≥ 6.75 hrs/night.
- **Workout scheduling rule:** prefer non-office days (Mon/Fri/Sat) for the home workout — office days have only 6 hrs sleep, which is the floor for exercise. If a workout has to happen on an office day, keep it short (15 min) and only if sleep was solid the night before.
- No caffeine after 6 PM on office days (11 PM bedtime); after 8 PM on non-office days (midnight bedtime). Hard cutoff.
- Every weekly plan file includes specific sleep guidance.

### Water — structured to 3 L/day (3.5 L in Delhi summer):

- 12 glasses (250 ml) spread across the day: waking, before each meal, before/after each walk, mid-morning, afternoon, evening.
- **Office days:** pack a 1 L bottle from home; refill once at office. Don't let office hours become a water gap.
- **Counts:** water, nimbu paani (no sugar), chaas, coconut water, herbal tea.
- **Doesn't count:** tea/coffee, milk, juice.
- Every weekly plan file includes the structured water schedule.
